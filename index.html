Clyde History

Endorsed by Walt Frazier, the Puma Clyde sis a hardwood classic sneaker that has long lived its presence onto the court and found a place on the everyday streets. Usually draped in a canvas upper, the shoe has found a place not only in basketball but subcultures among b-boys and in the hip-hop community.
